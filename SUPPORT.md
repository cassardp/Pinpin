## FAQ

**Q: How do I save content?**
A: Use the Share extension from any app (Safari, Photos, etc.) to save content to Pinpin.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes, Pinpin is offline-first. Your data is stored on your device and synchronized via your private iCloud account. We do not have access to your data.

**Q: How do I organize my pins?**
A: You can create categories and move your pins into them for better organization.
